AFRICA

Morocco

Morocco is an incredible country where you can live the 1001 arabian nights. Click here to know general things about the country and click here to plan your visit.

Top 5 places:
  1. Chefchauen
  2. Marrakech
  3. Merzouga dunes
  4. Fez
  5. Ait Ben Hadu
Must do:
  • Bargain in the street markets
  • Get lost in the medinas
  • Ride a camel
  • Sleep in a bedouin tent
  • Eat tagine and couscous dishes

Senegal

A trip to Senegal is like going back to our roots, where the tribal culture and the joy of the Senegalese people hooks you from the very first day. Click here to know general things about the country and click here to plan your visit.

Top 5 places:
  1. Dakar
  2. Saint Louis
  3. Touba
  4. Sine Saloum Delta
  5. Bassary Country
Must do:
  • Visit Ilee Goree (Dakar) to learn about the oirigins of slavery
  • Hike around the Pink Lake
  • Enjoy the long sandy beaches of the Petite Cote
  • Enjoy the Senegalese cuisine
  • Make a safari in any of the Natural Reserves of Senegal

ASIA

Vietnam

Vietnam is a busy country full of wonderfull landscapes, delicious food and nice people. Click here to know general things about the country and click here to plan your visit.

Top 5 places:
  1. Halong Bay
  2. Sapa
  3. Ho Chi Mihn
  4. Hoi An
  5. Hanoi
Must do:
  • Make a two-day hike surounded by rice fields and stay overnight with a local tribe
  • Take a boat and swim in Halong Bay
  • Enjoy the diverse and delicious street food you can fin in any corner (don't miss the Pho!)
  • Visit wonderfull temples in Hoi An
  • Enjoy the beaches and dives of the southern islands

AMERICA

Peru

Peru is a large country with wonderfull places to visit, from high mountains to the Amazonian Rainforest, including one of the seven wonders of the world. Click here to know general things about the country and click here to plan your visit.

Top 5 places:
  1. Machu Picchu
  2. Cuzco
  3. Arequipa
  4. Iquitos
  5. Puno
Must do:
  • Make the 4-day (or 2-day) trekking called "Camino Inca" to reach Machu Picchu
  • Reach Iquitos in a boat trip on the Amazonas river
  • Visit the Titicaca lake by boat (from Puno)
  • Enjoy the delicious Peruvian cuisine
  • Make a 2-day trip to the Amazonian Rainforest and enjoy its wildlife

Cuba

Travelling to Cuba is like travelling to the past, it is an amazing country full of color, joy and fiendly people. Click here to know general things about the country and click here to plan your visit.

Top 5 places:
  1. Habana
  2. Viñales
  3. Trinidad
  4. Santiago
  5. Varadero
Must do:
  • Cycle around in the area of Viñales surrounded by exotic nature
  • Dance salsa in one of the many events you can find every night in La Habana
  • Enjoy the endless sandy beach of Varadero
  • Dive in the idillic dive spots of the Caribean coast
  • Stay overnight in local houses and get to know their point of view about their situation (different at every house)
